# TLL Internship — Temasek Life Sciences Laboratory

**Duration:** May 13 – Aug 8, 2024
**PI:** Dr Shen Lisha | **Mentor:** Dr Fan Sheng
**Confidentiality:** Low (standard academic research)

## Focus

Investigating the phenotype of RRM1 (an RNA-binding protein involved in plant growth and development) and its interaction with downstream targets in Arabidopsis thaliana.

## Methods

- DNA extraction and PCR/gel electrophoresis for genotyping
- Plant crossing to generate double mutants (prr7 rrm1, prr9 rrm1)
- RNA-seq analysis for transcriptomics
- Agrobacterium-mediated transformation of plant cells
- Vector construction using restriction enzymes and ligation (PC300-35S-ECT-editing-FLAG)
- Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) for trichome morphology analysis
- RT-PCR and gene cloning for expression validation

## Key Results

- Identified homozygous double mutants and confirmed rrm1 leaf morphology (curly leaves, serrated edges)
- Generated transgenic plants carrying FLAG-tagged constructs (40/40 confirmed)
- Discovered MTB role in trichome development: mtb mutants showed 4–6 branched trichomes vs 3-branched wild-type

## Skills

PCR, RT-PCR, SEM, molecular cloning, genotyping, Agrobacterium transformation, RNA-seq, plant genetics, gel electrophoresis
