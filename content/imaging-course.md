# BN3406 — Biomedical Imaging and AI Applications

**Course:** BN3406, NUS Department of Biomedical Engineering
**Instructor:** Dr Lei Li, Digital Heart Lab

## Topics Covered

### Image Processing Fundamentals
- Image representation and digital coordinates
- Preprocessing: spatial normalisation, intensity/geometry transformations, convolution-based noise filtering
- Image segmentation: conventional methods and deep learning approaches
- 3D shape reconstruction from segmentation

### Registration
- Transformation models, similarity metrics, optimisation methods
- Deep learning registration: VoxelMorph (deformable image registration)

### Advanced AI
- Image synthesis/generation
- Self-supervised learning for medical imaging
- Explainable and generalisable AI

### Clinical Applications
- Echocardiography video segmentation
- Cardiac shape reconstruction

## Tools
Python, ITK-SNAP, Paraview

## Assessment
Mini-projects (image preprocessing, segmentation, registration) + group project applying AI to real-world healthcare imaging

## Skills Acquired
Image preprocessing, image segmentation, image registration, VoxelMorph, deep learning for medical imaging, self-supervised learning, 3D shape reconstruction, cardiac echo analysis
